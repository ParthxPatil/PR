🥗 Nutrition Tips

  • Stay hydrated – drink 8–10 glasses of water daily.
  • Consume adequate protein (0.8–1g per kg body weight).
  • Include complex carbs: oats, brown rice, sweet potatoes.
  • Avoid processed sugar and trans fats.
  • Eat 5–6 small meals instead of 3 large ones.
